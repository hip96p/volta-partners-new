About Volta Partners

Built from the inside out.

Volta Partners is an investment advisory and management consulting firm that helps investors and project sponsors turn promising opportunities into bankable investments across Africa and emerging markets.

What sets us apart is judgment and where we come from. Our founding team has worked inside the institutions that approve, structure, and finance Africa's largest investments — the African Development Bank, IFC, the World Bank, and British International Investment. We've sat in the investment committees. We know what it takes to get a project from concept to board approval to financial close.

We combine that institutional depth with investment banking execution, management consulting rigour, and on-the-ground knowledge across Francophone and Anglophone Africa. The result: advisory that doesn't just produce reports, but moves capital.
